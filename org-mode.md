Org-Mode
========
A note-taking and agenda building (from said notes) tool integrated into [[Emacs]].


Inline Formatting
-----------------
These are the standard Org inline styling syntaxes, although you can extend Org as well with your own:

-   `*Bold*`
-   `/​Italic/`
-   `_Underline_`
-   ​`~Code~`
-   `=​Verbatim=`
-   `+​Strikethrough+`
